    About Brand Spark - Kutty Intro

    We are an experienced startup organization established on April 14, 2024, in honor of our Creative Director's mother's birthday. We worked as freelancers for 4 years (from November 2020 to December 2023). It all started with routing COVID-19 patients to available beds in Chennai city through Instagram and Twitter. We also worked during the Chennai floods, coordinating emergency needs through Twitter with the Greater Chennai Corporation and police officials.
